About

I help people understand their money and feel confident managing it. I’ve spent the last decade working with investors at every stage of their financial journey.

I spent four years at Vanguard helping individuals and small business owners understand their money and make thoughtful plans for retirement and other goals. I also worked with financial advisors, helping them create simpler, lower-cost investment strategies for their clients.

After that, I spent four and a half years at Fidelity Investments working with high-net-worth individuals and families. I focused on helping people make confident financial decisions, understand their long-term plans, and organize things like investing, taxes, income and legacy planning. My advisory practice supported ongoing relationships of $600 million in client assets.

Today, I bring that experience into a warm, education-forward, advice-only practice designed to keep things clear, simple, and centered on what matters most to you.

FAQ

How do I pay?

Payment is collected at the time of booking through the scheduling link. The fee is non-refundable.

Can I reschedule?

You may reschedule up to two times at no additional cost, as long as changes are made at least 48 hours before your session. A third reschedule requires a new fee. Missed sessions without the required notice will result in a forfeited fee.

Do you manage investments?

No. Services are advice only. You keep full control and implement your own decisions at your own service provider.

Do you take custody or place trades?

No. The firm does not take custody, execute trades, or monitor accounts.
